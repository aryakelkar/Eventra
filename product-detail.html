Our Premium Vermicompost is a nutrient-rich organic fertilizer made from the finest earthworm castings. It enhances soil structure, improves water retention, and provides essential nutrients for healthy plant growth. Perfect for gardens, lawns, and potted plants.

Benefits:

  • 100% organic and chemical-free
  • Rich in beneficial microorganisms

  • Boosts plant immunity and growth

Usage:

Mix 1 part vermicompost with 3 parts soil for potting. For gardens, spread evenly and mix into the top 4-6 inches of soil. Apply every 4-6 weeks for best results.
